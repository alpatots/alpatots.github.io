Led the development of an internal LLM benchmarking platform used by dozens
of research teams running hundreds of evaluation jobs per day. The platform
was built on top of Azure ML as the compute provider and included a job
submission and management service, per-team visibility control with a way to
publish results to a global leaderboard, thousands of external and internal
evals to choose from, and a Python SDK to interact with it. The platform was
used for benchmarks during Phi-4 model training, and served as a gateway for
OpenAI launches on Azure AI Foundry.
