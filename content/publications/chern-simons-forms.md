*With Yuri Berest, Giovanni Felder, Ajay C. Ramadoss, and Thomas Willwacher.*

A sequel to the representation homology paper. Gives an explicit formula for
the "Drinfeld trace" map on derived representation schemes using Chern-Simons
classes of a canonical torsor. Also derives a combinatorial formula for a
canonical differential operator on symmetric algebras that appears in the
study of derived commuting schemes.
