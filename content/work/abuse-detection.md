Led the AI integrity engineering team in the Responsible AI organization and
managed a cross-functional squad focused on detecting and counteracting
malicious actors targeting AI systems on Azure OpenAI. Built detection,
investigation, and enforcement tooling that reduced 90th-percentile
time-to-action from weeks to under 24 hours. Scaled abuse-response operations
significantly and enabled same-day operational readiness for major model
launches. These tools enabled teams to discover and counteract many
large-scale AI security incidents.
