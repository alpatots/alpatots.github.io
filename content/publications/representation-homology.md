*With Yuri Berest, Giovanni Felder, Ajay C. Ramadoss, and Thomas Willwacher.*

Studies derived representation schemes parametrizing *n*-dimensional
representations of associative and Lie algebras. The homology of these
schemes is shown to be isomorphic to the Chevalley–Eilenberg homology of
certain Lie coalgebras. The paper constructs a "derived Harish-Chandra
homomorphism" and conjectures it is a quasi-isomorphism, drawing an analogy
with the classical Macdonald conjectures.
