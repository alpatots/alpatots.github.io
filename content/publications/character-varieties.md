*With Martin Kassabov.*

A short note showing that representation and character varieties of discrete
groups can be described as tensor products of functors over the PROP of
cocommutative Hopf algebras. This perspective provides a clean derivation of
representation homology and opens a path to constructing deformations of
character varieties relevant to quantum representations of mapping class
groups.
